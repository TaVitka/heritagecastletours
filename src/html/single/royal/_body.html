About this tour

Discover the elegance of Europe’s royal castles with guided tours that take you through magnificent halls and lush gardens. Explore the rich history and stunning architecture of each site.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris

  • Check-in at a luxury hotel.
  • Welcome dinner and tour briefing.

Day 2: Château de Chambord

  • Travel to the Loire Valley.
  • Guided tour of Château de Chambord, exploring its Renaissance architecture.
  • Free time to wander the gardens.

Day 3: Château de Chenonceau

  • Visit the iconic Château de Chenonceau, spanning the River Cher.
  • Guided tour and leisure time in the gardens.

Day 4: Château de Versailles

  • Return to Paris.
  • Full-day tour of the opulent Château de Versailles, including the Hall of Mirrors.

Day 5: Departure from Paris

  • Morning at leisure for shopping or sightseeing.
  • Departure.
