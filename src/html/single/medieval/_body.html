About this tour

Step back in time and explore the grandeur of medieval fortresses. Wander through ancient halls and battlements, learning about the history and legends from knowledgeable guides.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Munich

  • Check-in at a luxury hotel.
  • Welcome dinner and tour introduction.

Day 2: Neuschwanstein Castle

  • Travel to Bavaria.
  • Guided tour of Neuschwanstein Castle, enjoying the alpine scenery.

Day 3: Hohenzollern Castle

  • Visit Hohenzollern Castle atop Mount Hohenzollern.
  • Explore the museum with royal artifacts.

Day 4: Eltz Castle

  • Journey to the Moselle Valley.
  • Tour the well-preserved Eltz Castle and its surroundings.

Day 5: Return to Munich and Departure

  • Free time in Munich for additional sightseeing.
  • Departure.
